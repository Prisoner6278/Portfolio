Final Hour

Final Hour is a narrative driven 2D game made by Unity engine. The year is around 2030 and the world is about to end by a super-nova that is approximately 400 light years away. You are a young child that has not been socialized but finally decides to leave the terrible orphanage and explore the world.

As a Narrative Designer I wanted to show a story about a person who's freedom is going to be taken away as I was before(Koreans mandatory military service). However, that settings would not be relatable towards our target audiences are mostly English speaking audiences and they don't have the draft currnetly.

So to be bit more relatable, we switched the settings to fictional American city and a situation that your "freedom" is taken away. Far worse draft but still posibilities that could happen in real life.

At first I wanted to make realistic people with different cruel stories of them. However, our target audience were all ages so we had to tone it down. We also wanted to be kids friendly so we made a fictional species that acts like a human as well.
