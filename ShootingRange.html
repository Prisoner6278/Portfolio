Shooting Range

Shooting Range This is a shooting range game where the player and the targets spawn location is randomized and all the targets including player, moves to a random position as well. The only thing that you need to rely on, is your mouse aim.

My Experiences

Most of the assets are either built in to Unity or has been imported. Almost everything is randomized. The level, enemy spawn location, player spawn location, and targets/player movement as well. I chose randomness because I didn't wanted to give player the control other than their aim.
