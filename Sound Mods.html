War Thunder Sound Mod

The more update that War Thunder did, I disliked the tank crew voice lines more. So I decided to make my own. At first it was really hard to figure it out since War Thunder only showed how to implement the sound mods not how to create it.

I started to figure out simple stuff, starting with changing background music. Switching to classical rock music to my likes gave me more fun and personal experience like topgun. I also wanted to swtich the crew voice to one of my favorite game characters and somehow, I managed to figure it out by myself through experimenting. Now, I can not only change crew and music, but almost all sounds in game.
