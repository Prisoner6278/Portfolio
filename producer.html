Case studies

01) Milestone planning & scheduling (Producer)

  • Scale: Team 12 (Narrative 3, Art 4, Programmer 2, Sound 2, Producer 1)
  • Context: Needed a clear plan to coordinate multiple workstreams with shared dependencies.
  • Actions: Broke down work into trackable items, sequenced dependencies, and scheduled milestones on a timeline.
  • Impact: Decreased the frequency of milestone delays by making scope changes explicit and owned.
  • Artifacts: Milestone definitions + acceptance criteria, dependency notes, Jira Timeline (anonymized).

02) Scope & dependency reset

  • Scale: Duration 6 months / Milestones 4
  • Context: Competing priorities and unclear dependency order were causing drift and rework.
  • Actions: Mapped dependencies, defined cut lines (in/out of scope), and re-baselined milestones with owners and criteria — including removing several non-critical characters to reduce workload.
  • Impact: Reduced workload through scope cuts to ship on time while maintaining clarity around “done” for each milestone.
  • Artifacts: Dependency map snapshot, milestone sheet (owners + criteria + cut lines), decision log excerpt.

03) Decision & stakeholder alignment cadence

  • Scale: Stakeholders: Lead Narrative, Programmer, Art / Update cadence: weekly
  • Context: Information was scattered across tools and conversations; decisions stayed open too long.
  • Actions: Standardized weekly updates around the CSV → Unity content pipeline (what changed / what’s at risk / what decisions are needed) and maintained a decision log.
  • Impact: Reduced cross-team back-and-forth and unblocked content integration by clarifying ownership, acceptance criteria, and decision closure for the CSV → Unity handoff.
  • Artifacts: Weekly update format, decision log excerpt, RAID-style risk list (incl. CSV → Unity integration risks).
